Life is like a journey to find happiness. And we either select a way among many options or explore a new way. Some people opt for a straight road, which can be considered as a monotonous trip. Others decide to choose a difficult, treacherous path because they enjoy a challenge. Some even go to the wilderness which is hard to call a road. No road, however, can be said to be the solution, and no matter which way you turn, you can find your own results and happiness.

This exhibit can be viewed in any way that you choose. You can read someone's happiness along the road, or you can collect your own random fragments of joy. I wish you can find happiness on each and every journey.

* You can stop participating in this exhibition at any time as long as you find your happiness! :)

It is the law of this world that water will one day becomes part of the ocean. Every moments that have passed will surely remain to me, to you, and to us and become the ocean. The sea turns into waves to help row, becomes salt to become nutrients and transforms into various shapes to colorfully change the world. It naturally left my hand, but I capture my 10 years, Dec-ade, which flowed and flowed in each shape and took on various colors and forms.
